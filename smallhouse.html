Small House

  • Farmer cheese - 0.5 kg
  • Honey graham crackers - 18 pcs.
  • Sugar - 8 tablespoons
  • Butter - 150 g
  • Cocoa - 4 tablespoons
  • Milk - 200 g
  • Raisins, goji
  1. Mix 100 g of butter with 3 tablespoons of sugar, add farmer cheese, rub well. If the farmer cheese is dry, add some milk.
  2. Lay the foil on a flat long dish. Pour some cold milk into a bowl. Quickly dip the cookies one by one in cold milk and lay them out on the foil in three rows - 3x3.
  3. Put half of the curd cream on cookies, drown raisins, goji (any berries to taste or add nothing) into the cream on the cookies.
  4. Put the next layer of cookies dipped in milk.
  5. In the remaining half of the farm cheese cream add 1-2 tablespoons of cocoa, mix and put on cookies.
  6. Form a "house": with the help of foil, lift the two extreme rows of cookies and connect the edges into a triangle, pressing the foil lightly. Cool down.
  7. Prepare chocolate cream: in a saucepan, mix 5 tablespoons of sugar, 2 tablespoons of cocoa, 5-6 tablespoons of milk. Boil until thickened, add 50 g of butter, mix and cool.
  8. Pour the chocolate cream over the cake and refrigerate.
